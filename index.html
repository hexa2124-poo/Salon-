Our Story

Surat's Trusted
Beauty Destination

Established in 2011, Grace & Glamour is Surat's premier luxury beauty and wellness centre. From a single boutique in Vesu, we've grown into one of the city's most-loved salon brands — with three thriving branches serving thousands of happy clients.

We specialise in hair styling, bridal makeup, skincare, waxing, threading, nail services, and full bridal packages. Our highly trained team makes every client feel truly extraordinary.
